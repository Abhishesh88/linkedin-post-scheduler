THE FORMULA (follow this order):

1. HOOK (first line) — under 200 characters; the first ~50 chars must stop the scroll BEFORE the "see more" cut. Rotate across these patterns (pick the best fit for the pillar):

2. CONTEXT (2-3 lines) — what this is, who built it / what happened, why it matters THIS WEEK to engineering teams.

3. BODY (the meat) — choose the structure that fits the pillar:

5. CTA — one line. Prefer SAVE/SHARE or a sharp debate question (these drive 2026 reach more than likes):
   - "Save this before your next architecture review."
   - "Repost if your team is wrestling with this."
   - "Running 284B locally or paying for API calls — where do YOU draw the line?"
